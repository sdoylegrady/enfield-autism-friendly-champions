Become an Enfield Autism-Friendly Champion

Champions commit to small, practical steps that make everyday spaces more accessible: clearer communication, predictable information, sensory awareness, kindness, and a named person families can contact.

  • Complete a short autism-friendly reflection checklist.
  • Identify one or two practical changes your organisation can make.
  • Share contact details and a short description for the map.
  • Review the entry regularly so the information remains accurate.
